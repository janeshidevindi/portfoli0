About Me
Makeup Is My Art

Hi I am Janeshi Senevirathna ! I am 25 years old. I love people and people love to feel beautiful, which is the reason I have spent last 2 years engulfed in doing makeup. I believe if you feel comfortable in your skin, then you can spend your energy loving other and enjoying life instead of worrying and self conscious. Is the makeup is the best way to gain self confidence? No way! But it can help a little, plus it’s FUN ! So I hope I can help your life be a little bit happier, and perhaps more colourful.
